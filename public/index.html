Episode 1: Disco Jesus Saves

Show Notes:

Don Denton interviews Vaughn Matthew Irving about his upcoming Captital Fringe Festival show, Disco Jesus on the Apostles of Funk. Get a little insight into the rigors and rewards of creating and producing a brand new, actor-musician musical.
